Are plans commitment-free?

You decide: you can pay monthly, and commit on a month-by-month basis or you can pay yearly, and commit for 12 months following your subscription.

‍

Are there any limits to my 14-day free-trial period?

Your free trial has no limit in terms of signatures or documents. However, you will be limited to 10 users for the duration of the free trial. Once regular billing begins, you’ll be able to add as many users as you need.

‍

Are signatures collected during my 14-day free-trial period legally-binding?

Yes. Every single signature processed through our app complies to security norms and generate legally-binding proof of agreement.

‍

When do payments start?

During or at the end of your free trial, you’ll be able to select the plan and the number of users that you wish to invite to use the platform. You’ll then be billed once your trial has expired.

‍

How can I pay?

In the app, you’ll be able to set up credit card payments. Get in touch with our team if you’d prefer to be billed using a different payment method.

‍

How will I be billed?

If you’ve picked monthly billing, you will be billed each month. If you’ve selected annual billing, you will pay your yearly amount upfront.
